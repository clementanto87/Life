Using commas:
	1. Use comma after the dependent clause. If the dependent clause comes in the first part of the sentence.
	2. comma must be used between the two independent clauses in complex sentence.
	3. Use comma to seperate the item in the list.
	4. Use comma with the cohesive devices, Ex: Firstlly, To begin with,  Finally, As a  consequence, 

Apostrophes:
	1. Possessives -> to represent it her's Ex: It is the government's resposibility.
	2. Contraction -> to avoid use of is Ex: Mary's here


Possessives:
	boss'
	parents'

Capital letters:
	1. Starting of the sentence
	2. Place, name, institutions (Proper noun)
