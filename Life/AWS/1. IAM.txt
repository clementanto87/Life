IAM - Identity and access management - level of access to aws console
	- Centralise control on the access of aws account
	- Shared access to aws account 
	- Granular access to aws account
	- Identity federation 
	- MFA (multi factor authentication)
	- Provide temporary access to the user
	- Password rotation policy
	

Terminology:

User:
	People or employee of the organization 
Group:
	Collection of user, each group will have different level of access
Policies:
	are in JSON formate, it is permission given to user and group
Roles:
	Role to access different resource in the AWS
